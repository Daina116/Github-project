Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invite team members to my workspace?

To invite team members:

  1. Navigate to the Team page
  2. Click the "Invite Members" button
  3. Enter email addresses of team members
  4. Select their role and permissions
  5. Click "Send Invitations"

Team members will receive an email invitation to join your workspace.

What are the different user roles and permissions?

Our platform offers several user roles:

  • Admin: Full access to all features and settings
  • Manager: Can manage projects and team members
  • Member: Can participate in projects and access shared resources
  • Viewer: Read-only access to specific content
How do I set up video conferences?

To set up video conferences:

  1. Go to the Video page
  2. Click "Start New Meeting" or schedule one
  3. Configure meeting settings (title, duration, participants)
  4. Share the meeting link with participants
  5. Join the meeting at the scheduled time
How secure is my data on the platform?

We take data security seriously:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • GDPR and CCPA compliant
  • Two-factor authentication available
  • Regular data backups
Can I integrate with other tools and services?

Yes! We support integrations with popular tools:

  • Slack, Microsoft Teams
  • Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive
  • Jira, Trello, Asana
  • Calendar apps (Google Calendar, Outlook)
  • Custom API integrations
What are the pricing plans available?

We offer flexible pricing plans:

  • Free: Up to 5 users, basic features
  • Pro: $10/user/month, advanced features
  • Business: $25/user/month, full features + priority support
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing, dedicated support
